Plain English Summary: We collect information you give us when signing up and using the platform. We use it to run LoadLeader, connect pilots with carriers, and improve our service. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

1. Overview

LoadLeader ("we," "us," or "our") operates loadleaderservice.com and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

By using LoadLeader, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect pilots with carriers and facilitate job matching
  • Display your profile to other users as appropriate
  • Send transactional emails (application notifications, job acceptances, account alerts)
  • Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Provide AI-powered search and matching features
  • Improve the Platform and develop new features
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send platform updates and newsletters (you can opt out at any time)

Phone Number Disclosure

Your phone number is only shared with another user when a carrier explicitly accepts your pilot application or when you accept a pilot as a carrier. Phone numbers are never displayed publicly on the Platform.

4. How We Share Your Information

With Other Users

Your profile information (name, rating, state, certifications, bio) is visible to other LoadLeader users. Your contact information is only shared when both parties have confirmed a job.

With Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the Platform:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Render — backend hosting
  • Netlify — website hosting
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI-powered features
  • Gmail/Google — email notifications

These providers are contractually bound to use your data only to provide services to LoadLeader.

We Do Not Sell Your Data

LoadLeader does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Period.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights and safety of LoadLeader, our users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing via Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore security rules restricting data access
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI compliant)
  • Regular security reviews

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you suspect a security breach, contact us immediately at support@loadleaderservice.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

We may retain certain information after account deletion for:

  • Legal compliance (up to 7 years for financial records)
  • Fraud prevention
  • Resolving disputes
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics (indefinitely, as it cannot identify you)

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update inaccurate information from your profile settings
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Portability — Request an export of your data in a common format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@loadleaderservice.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. You may request disclosure of personal information collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information). Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Cookies & Tracking

LoadLeader uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze Platform usage and performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Platform functionality including the ability to stay logged in.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels for advertising purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

LoadLeader is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us at support@loadleaderservice.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
