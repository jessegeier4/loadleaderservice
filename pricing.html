Common Questions
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — cancel anytime from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees ever.
Does LoadLeader take a cut of my earnings? +
No. Your subscription is a flat monthly fee. LoadLeader never takes a percentage of what you earn per job — 100% of what a carrier pays goes to you.
What happens after my carrier free trial ends? +
After 30 days you'll be prompted to subscribe to Carrier Pro to continue posting loads. Your existing loads and data are saved. You can also export your history at any time.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment? +
No contracts. Monthly plans are month-to-month. Annual plans are billed once per year and save you 20% compared to monthly.
Can I switch plans? +
Yes — upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade you get immediate access. When you downgrade the change takes effect at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 7-day money back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied in your first 7 days contact support for a full refund.
